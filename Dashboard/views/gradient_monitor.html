Gradient Health Troubleshooting

Vanishing Gradients — Early layer grads near zero. Fixes: use LayerNorm (not BatchNorm), proper init (xavier/gaussian), skip connections, reduce depth.
Exploding Gradients — Grad norms > 10 for any layer. Fixes: gradient clipping (max_norm=1.0), lower LR, better init, add residual scaling (1/sqrt(N)).
Healthy Flow — Gradients decrease smoothly from later to earlier layers. Variance stays within 0.1-10 range across all layers.
Dead Neurons — Activations all negative (ReLU dies) or all zero. Fixes: use LeakyReLU/GELU, check bias init, ensure sufficient gradient flow.
NaN/Inf — Silently kills training. Fixes: add torch.isnan check in training loop, gradient clipping, lower LR, check for log(0) or division by zero.
